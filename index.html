Kangyou Yu

Hi, this is Kangyou, a junior undergraduate student from Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University in China and the University of Liverpool in the UK, expecting to get my Bachelor degree in Information and Computing Science in June, 2022.

I work as a remote research intern in the VVISE Lab, supervised by Prof. Wolfgang Stuerzlinger (Simon Fraser University, Canada). I also work as a research assistant at the X-CHI Lab of Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University since 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Hai-Ning Liang and Prof. Wenge Xu (will start his lecturer job at Birmingham City University).

I am interested in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), AR, VR, MR, and Exergames. I am looking for an exalting Research-based Master or PhD position of HCI and XR in 2022 fall.
